Our Journey in Automotive Cybersecurity

From pioneering research to industry leadership, our evolution mirrors the transformation of the automotive industry itself

2018

Foundation & Vision

Founded by automotive security pioneers with a vision to protect the emerging software-defined vehicle ecosystem. Initial focus on CAN bus security research.

2019

First AI Solution Launch

Launched Automator AI, the first machine learning-powered automotive threat detection system. Secured initial partnerships with Tier 1 suppliers.

2020

Industry Recognition

Achieved ASPICE Level 3 certification and established partnerships with major OEMs. Recognized as "Automotive Cybersecurity Innovator of the Year."

2022

Complete Platform Launch

Launched comprehensive six-product ecosystem including IDPS, vSOC, and OTA Updater. Reached 1 million vehicles protected milestone.

2024

Global Market Leadership

Protecting over 2.8 million vehicles globally. Established international presence with offices in Detroit, Stuttgart, and Tokyo.

2025 & Beyond

Next-Generation Security

Pioneering quantum-resistant cryptography and autonomous vehicle security frameworks. Leading the industry into the fully autonomous future.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide our mission to protect lives through automotive cybersecurity excellence

Safety First

Every decision we make prioritizes the safety of drivers, passengers, and pedestrians. We understand that automotive cybersecurity isn't just about protecting data—it's about protecting lives on the road.

Proactive Defense

We believe in preventing threats before they materialize. Our AI-powered systems continuously learn and adapt, staying ahead of emerging attack vectors in the rapidly evolving automotive landscape.

Intelligent Automation

Human expertise enhanced by machine intelligence. Our solutions automate complex security processes while maintaining human oversight for critical decisions, ensuring both efficiency and reliability.

Automotive systems demand 99.99% uptime. Our solutions are built with redundancy, failsafes, and continuous monitoring to ensure they perform flawlessly when lives depend on them.

Continuous Learning
